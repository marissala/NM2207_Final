F O R E S T

Each of us has a way of organising thoughts, people, events, emotions, in our heads. Our feeling of identity is linked to both fitting in with others but also having a sense of being unique.

A way to visualize who we are internally, up in our heads, is by imagining that our minds are like forests.

Some people's mind forests might be filled with short trees, all in line. Other's might have a chaotic mess on one end and a neatly clean park on the other end.

The colors of our leaves might be from the same color pallet, or every tree might carry a different color. To finegrade intricate details in our mind-forests, the leaves on the trees can also vary in their size or form.

But, instead of imagining what your mind forest would be like - how about drawing it out on the canvas provided here?
